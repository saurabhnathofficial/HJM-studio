01

STRATEGY

During the Discovery phase, we embark on a comprehensive journey to understand your brand, objectives, industry landscape, and target audience. I create visual mood boards to capture the essence of the project and define the typography, color palette, and motion elements that will breathe life into the design. I determine the most effective strategies for presenting the content of your site, ensuring visitors can effortlessly navigate and engage with your content.

02

Design

In the Design phase, creativity takes center stage as I define your brand identity through a cohesive visual design and user experience. and transform ideas into visual concepts with bespoke layouts, wireframes and site components. In this phase I prioritize user experience and design consistency, ensuring that every design element serves a purpose and resonates with your target audience. This phase is where ideas evolve into tangible assets that set the stage for development.

03

development

With meticulous attention to detail and unwavering quality commitment, I breathe life into your project on the chosen platform, be it Squarespace or another CMS.

04

hand off

I conduct comprehensive testing to ensure that everything is functioning smoothly, meeting accessibility requirements and responsive on all device sizes. Once approved, I provide you with all the necessary resources and documentation, empowering you to take full control of your website.
